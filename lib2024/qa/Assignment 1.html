My Favourite Movie: The Princess Bride

The Princess Bride is 1987 Fantasy Adventure Comedy. The main plot of the movie, which told by a Grandfather reading his sick Grandson a story, is about a farmhand named Westley who must rescue his true love Princess Buttercup from the evil Prince Humperdinck

Cast List:

Fred Savage As The Grandson
Peter Falk as The Grandpa
Cary Elwes as Westley
Robin Wright as Buttercup
